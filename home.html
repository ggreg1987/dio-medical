Dr. Armin Salimi completed his bachelor degree in biological science from University of Maryland,
and earned his Master degree in biotechnology from John Hopkins University.
He completed Doctorate of Dental Surgery from the University Of Maryland School Of Dentistry.
He served as oral surgery clerk in the emergency clinic of University of Maryland.
While he was a student, he remained involved in serving the community as a volunteer
in emergency department of Suburban hospital in Bethesda Maryland.
Dr. Salimi is an active member in many dental societies, including the:

  • American Academy of Implant Dentistry
  • American Dental Association
  • Academy of General Dentistry
  • American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry.
  • Maryland Dental Association
Dr. Salimi regularly attends the major dental conferences annually to keep abreast
of the latest procedures, materials and technology that he can apply to his practice.
He enjoys spending time with family and friends and swimming as his professional sport.
